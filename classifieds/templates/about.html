What's CC Swapr?

During 5th block of this year, I was in a class called Database Systems. While working on my project one night Stanley Carson suggested that I build a Craigslist-like website for Colorado College where students could trade books, sell miscellaneous things, organize block break trips and rides, publicize events, or just discuss hot topics.

So that's what I did. Two hours later I had a simple prototype of the site and now it's ready to go!

Remember, I'm not perfect, and neither is the site. So if you see something that could be better let me know and I'll work on it.
